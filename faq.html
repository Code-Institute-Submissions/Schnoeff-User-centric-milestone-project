Frequently asked questions:

  • Do you have any alternative milks?

    Yes! We have a wide range of milks thats can be used upon request. These include but are not limited to: Soya, Almond, Coconut, Lacto free and many more!

  • Is there a loyalty scheme?

    At this present time we unfortunately do not have any loyalty scheme in place however we do plan to add one in the future!

  • Can you ask for takeaway?

    Certainly! You are free to take whatever you want away with you

  • Are there any parking facilities nearby?

    Due to our location, parking can sometimes be a struggle depending on the time of day. However, there is a car park that is about a 2-3 minute walk from the coffee shop.

  • Do you have any vegan options?

    We always have multiple vegan options on offer however some bakery items do change.

  • Is there disabled access?

    Yes! The enterance to our shop is just up a little ramp and the builidng is very spacious allowing for easy access around the shop.

  • Are there baby changing facilities?

    We have baby changing facilities located next to the toilets.

  • Do you have free Wifi?

    We have free, fast Wifi on sight with no signup required.
